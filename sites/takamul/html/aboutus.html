Company Overview

Takamul Project Management Company (Takamul) is a specialized, integrated project management firm dedicated to promoting the project management practice in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

In 2011, Salem H. Shaheen, and in cooperation with prominent Saudi businessman and project management professionals, founded Takamul to promote professional project management in Saudi Arabia and provide independent expertise, opinion and advice to public and private projects owners in Saudi Arabia and in GCC, starting with the King Abdullah City for Atomic and Renewable Energy (KA-CARE) project in Riyadh.

Takamul assists corporate, governmental, and private clients across various industries to complete their projects On-Time and Within Budget with Right Scope and Required Quality, thus maximizing the return on their investments. Originally established to promote the project management profession combining international experience with local knowledge that fully understands the culture of Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region countries and the characteristics of the construction industry herein.

Takamul provides start-to-end professional program and project management consulting services working closely with our valued clients. We offer full project management solutions tailor made to suit each client with innovative approach. Our consulting services include the entire project lifecycle, starting from project development, scoping, pre-feasibility and feasibility studies, detailed design, tendering and procurement, contract management, construction management as well as commissioning and hand-over.

Takamul recognizes that the quality of our services hinges on the people who deliver it. Takamul’s team are passionate, driven professionals with initiative and hands-on experience in project management. As well as specialist in project management skills, Takamul’s team have a range of experience gained from having worked in complementary fields such as development, engineering consultancy, construction, infrastructure, and asset ownership.

With Takamul’s top management commitment, our team pride themselves on their energy, initiative and passion, and strives to deliver the best outcome for our clients, the project and the end user's on every project Takamul works on.
