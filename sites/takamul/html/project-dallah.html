Dallah South Residence

Location
Al-Nakheel District, Riyadh, KSA
Client
Dallah Healthcare Company
Scope of Services:

Management of design and construction of Dallah Hospital South Medical Staff Residential Project with 51 residential units, one recreational area (500m²), 326 parking spaces, one sewage treatment plant and raw water tank. The project also have three basements for parking, 3.5 floors – two apartment buildings, and 2.5 floors – four villa buildings at BUA of 29,539m² on a 6,250m² land.

Project Value
SAR 77 million
