Assessment Of Gasco Projects

Location
Riyadh, KSA
Client
National Gas & Industrialization Co.
Scope of Services:

​An independent third party assessment of the ongoing GASCO projects specially the project for construction of the “New and Upgrading Loading / Unloading Bays and Spherical Storage Tanks Gauging System”. The assessment included evaluation of the project current status and identify critical issues and risks. Also, it included assessing the performance of both the contractor and the consultant. Takamul prepared a detailed report on the findings, available courses of action and the recommended plan for rectification of the project delay and save on cost.

Project Value
SR 161,550,000
