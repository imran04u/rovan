Our Vision and Mission

In today’s complex world, project management is a really hot topic, as modern projects are no longer about stacking up a pile of bricks, putting out a shiny silver mailbox and planting a few petunias.

What is needed in terms of construction, communication and environment are so complex that specialty tools and skills are required to bring modern mega projects to life. Takamul was formed through a collaboration of project management professionals, engineers, and prominent businessmen, to promote the growing importance of the project management profession.

The word “Takamul” in English means integrated, and providing integrated project management solutions is the flagship service of our company. The Kingdom and the region are investing vast amounts of capital in energy, healthcare, housing, education, hospitality, retail, sports and related infrastructure projects, along with the country’s commitment to its ambitious projects under the Vision 2030 plan and National Transformation Program 2020; Takamul is dedicated to ensure innovative and flexible solutions that exceed projects stakeholders expectations.

Our Vision

“To become the leading project management company in the region, known for its excellence, exceptional project and program portfolio, professionalism and client focused approach”

Our Mission

“To promote program and project management best practices in the Kingdom through the delivery of built assets on time, within budget, and with the highest quality that exceed client expectations”

Our Values

Our core values reflect what is truly important to Takamul as an organization. These are not values that change from time to time, situation-to-situation or person-to-person, but rather they are the underpinning of our culture from day one:

Client Focus

Clients are the essence of our business, and our most important stakeholders.

Time and Budget

We are committed to the highest levels of integrity, transparency and clarity in communicating with our stakeholders.

Integrity and Transparency

Time is money, and an asset delivered on time and within the set budget will result on greater returns. We strive to exert our best efforts to project delivery on- time and within set budgets.

Innovation and Differentiation

We foster partnerships & alliances that enable us to remain at the forefront of our industry, by creating new, unique and innovative services that add value to our clients.

Empowering our People

Our success is conveyed through the collective energy, drive and intelligence our Team. We see to it that we create a work environment where motivated Team Members can flourish and succeed to their highest potential. We appreciate effort and reward results.

Profits and Growth

We recognize that profits are essential for growth but also for, prosperity and social welfare.
