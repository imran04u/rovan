Takamul Project Management Company (Takamul) is a specialized, integrated project management firm dedicated to promoting the project management practice in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Takamul assists corporate, governmental, and private clients across various industries to complete their projects On-Time and Within Budget with Right Scope and Required Quality maximizing the return on their investments. Originally established to promote the project management profession combining international experience with local knowledge that is fully understands the culture of Saudi Arabia and the Gulf region countries and the characteristics of the construction industry herein.

About Company

After many decades of working with mega projects in Saudi Arabia, it came to my attention that there is a vital need for the practice of project management profession in the Kingdom and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) as well.
