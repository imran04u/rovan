Since its establishment in 2011, Takamul Project Management has grown into a leading PM/CM (Project Management/ Construction Management) company in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We offer full innovative and tailored project management solutions to suit each of our client's needs. We serve clients from among corporate, governmental and private sectors across various industries to optimize the use of their resources by carefully managing different stages of their projects starting from the feasibility study, to master planning, design, procurement and construction all the way to commissioning and project close out.

Takamul employs highly qualified project management professionals, implements best project management practices and apply lessons learned from previous projects in KSA to deliver projects On-Time, within Budget and Required Quality.
